Why use it?


In medicine, many diagnoses are based on medical images. However, for the same patient, many images are taken at different moments of time, different positions and also from different devices. These differences in acquisition give rise to images with important geometric differences that prevent them from precisely fitting one over the other. Regim fuses these images, eliminating geometric differences so that pixels located in the same coordinates correspond to the same point of the object and, therefore, both images can be easily compared or integrated.
